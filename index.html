Privacy Policy for Morser Discord Bot

Last updated: August 15, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy of the Morser Discord bot. This bot is designed to convert text to Morse code and vice versa. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy outlines how we handle your data.

2. Information We Collect

Morser does not collect or store any personal information. The bot processes text messages to convert them into Morse code or decode Morse code into text. No data is retained after processing.

3. How We Use Your Information

Morser only uses the data you provide to perform the requested conversions. No information is stored, shared, or used for any other purpose.

4. Data Security

We prioritize your privacy and ensure that no data is stored or transmitted to third parties. The bot operates within the Discord platform, and no external storage or processing is involved.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here, and the date of the latest update will be indicated at the top of the policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at Dever Labs.
